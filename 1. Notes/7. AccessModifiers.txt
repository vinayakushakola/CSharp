Access Modifiers
0. Private
1. Protected
2. Internal
3. Protected Internal 
4. Public

Private - Only with in the containing class.
Public - Anywhere, No Restricvtions.
Protected - With in the containing types and the types derived from the containing type.
Internal - Anywhere with in the containing assembly.
Protected Internal - Anywhere in the containing assembly and from within a derived class in any another assembly.
